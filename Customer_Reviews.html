Customer Reviews

Walter White

Let me tell you about my visit to Los Pollos Hermanos, where I had a downright surreal experience. The chicken was good, no doubt about it – crispy, flavorful, and all that. But what really struck me was the owner, Gustavo Fring. I couldn't shake the feeling that he's not just a fast-food guy. There was this air of control and calculation about him that made me wonder if there's more to this place than meets the eye. I mean, I'm just a chemistry teacher, but even I could sense there's some serious business happening behind those kitchen doors. So yeah, if you're into chicken and you don't mind a side of intrigue, Los Pollos Hermanos might be your spot. Just don't be surprised if things get a little, well, "blue."
