About Us - Los Pollos Hermanos

Mr. Gustavo Fring : Co-Founder and Owner

Welcome to Los Pollos Hermanos, where flavor and quality meet in perfect harmony. Established in 1986, we've been serving our community with the most delectable, mouthwatering dishes that have become synonymous with our name.

Our Story:

Los Pollos Hermanos began as a humble dream of two brothers, Max and Gustavo, who shared a deep passion for culinary excellence and a commitment to providing an unforgettable dining experience. Their vision was simple yet profound - to create a haven for those who seek authentic, flavorful, and satisfying meals that bring families and friends together.

Our Philosophy:

At Los Pollos Hermanos, we believe that the heart of any great meal lies in the ingredients we use and the care with which we prepare our dishes. We source the freshest, highest quality ingredients to ensure that every bite you take is a testament to our dedication to excellence. Our culinary team combines traditional recipes with innovative techniques, resulting in dishes that pay homage to our rich heritage while embracing modern flavors.

The Taste Experience:

From our signature grilled chicken, marinated to perfection in a secret blend of herbs and spices, to our mouthwatering sides that range from crispy golden fries to creamy coleslaw, every dish at Los Pollos Hermanos is crafted to tantalize your taste buds. Our commitment to culinary excellence extends to our vegetarian and vegan options, ensuring that every guest can find a dish that resonates with their preferences.

Visit Us:

We invite you to join us on this journey of flavor, community, and joy. Come experience the magic that happens when passion meets a plate, only at Los Pollos Hermanos.
